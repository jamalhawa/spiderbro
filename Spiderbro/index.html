About

Spiderbro is a teenage/young adult male with superpowers that resemble a spider. Everything known about him is simply speculation, as nobody truly knows his identity. Although, He has been spotted with a woman with red hair from time to time. Other than that however, she is the only lead we have pertaining to his identity.

Powers

As stated before, his powers resemble a spider. These include, but are not limited to extreme athleticism, immense strength relative to bodyweight, a “spidey-sense”, sticky fingers and feet, and web slinging. Speculators link this to a “radioactive spider bite”, but that makes little sense. To be fair though, not much about him “makes sense”. He has a separate power, perhaps linked or unlinked to his other ones. Spiderbro has a remarkable metabolism. In other words, he can eat ANYTHING. Despite the fact his diet consists only of fast food, he doesn’t seem to gain weight. He also metabolizes alcohol like its water. He is known to drink multiple cases of it before tapping out.

Physicals

He is about 5'8”-5'10”, but if anyone were to ask him, he would insist that he is above six feet. And strangely, whenever an attractive female reporter asks him his height, the number moves up to 6’3”. He looks to be in great shape–perhaps swinging off buildings all day is great exercise. Overall he has a good upper body. His legs could definitely use work though. At the various campus gyms he is spotted at, he never hits his legs, rather opting for chest and bicep work.

Profile

Spiderbro’s hobbies include partying, playing American Football, jumping off of buildings, jumping onto buildings, and trying to pick up women (keyword is trying). When he isn’t fighting forces of evil, Spiderbro is frequently caught at parties getting “crunked”. As previously mentioned, he also enjoys going to the gym from time to time. He can’t actually benefit from our “normal human” weights and machines due to his super strength, so it is presumed he simply goes to show off his incredible feats of power, such as his 10 plate bench press. He doesn’t shake your hand or look you in the eye, rather, his preferred form of greeting is a fist bump and/or a “whats good bruh”. He also never cleans up after his webs, always makes sure to keep his elbows ON the table, and at the end of the day, after beating up some bad guys, he always puffs on his mango vape. You know, if he didn’t save our city here and there, he would kind of be a douche…

Enemies

Although Spiderbro usually focuses on smaller crimes, he has been taken to his limits by a few foes. Most notably his polar opposite, Venom. Venom abides traffic laws, is quite tidy and organized, and NEVER forgets to say please or thank you. Aside from the villainy and malice he participates in, Venom actually seems like a well rounded guy. Alas, Spiderman has bested him in their conflicts, keeping our city safe.

The Red Haired Woman

Despite his elusive nature, he seems to fixate on one civilian. A lovely young woman with red hair. In various Spiderbro fanclubs, they refer to her as “Mary Jane”, due to her vivid green eyes resembling the color of mary jane–the drug. Poetic. He seems to regularly swing with her and prioritizes her in zones of conflict. Due to this, there have been questions brought up about a romantic relationship. Efforts are being made everyday in order to find this woman’s identity, as she is the key to unveiling the mask of the Spiderbro.

Relation with Football

Another lead to Spiderbro’s identity could be football for a couple reasons. He is often spotted playing at local campuses and parks. Also, Spiderbro came into existence only after prominent highschool football coach, Mark Ben (referred to by some of his players as “Uncle” or “Uncle Ben”), was murdered while getting robbed for his wallet. He was like family to his players, and was known and loved around his community. Currently, the entire former football roster is being put under immense questioning by fellow Spiderbro enthusiasts. I personally think it's far-fetched, but it's cool to think the tragic death of a coach sparked the comeuppance of our beloved hero.
